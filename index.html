Cats!

Persian cats were the first cat breed to be on display at cat shows. They are the most popular cat breed in the US, this happed after they were brought to the US in 1906 and farmers quite enjoyed their company. Dispite their looks, persians are actually very low maintenance. Their coats also come in all sorts of colors, patterns, and sizes.

Maine Coon cats are often mistake as bobcats for their size, they are among one of the largest cat breeds in the world. Maine Coon's are always ready to take on the snow because thats what they were bred for, The thick coat keeps their body warm while they are in colder weather like snow. Dispite being known for their brown coats and racoon like apperances, their coats arent always brown. Maine Coons were the first cats to win at the most famous cat show in the US. A Maine Coon cat was the first ever animal to be cloned commerically.
